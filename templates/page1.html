How It Works

A completely unnecessary explanation of a completely unnecessary game.

• The grid is 3×3, so there are 9 boxes.
• One box is secretly chosen as the “winning” box on the server side.
• When you click a box, the app checks if your choice matches the winning index.
• If you’re wrong, you get a configurable roast. If you’re right, you get a configurable win message.

That’s it. There are no points, no rewards, and no scoreboard.
Just vibes and bad decisions.
